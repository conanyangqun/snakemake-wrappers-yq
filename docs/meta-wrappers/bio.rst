.. _`Bio`:



Bio 
============

Curated and tested combinations of bioinformatics wrappers that fulfil common tasks with popular bioinformatics tools, in a best-practice way.
For using them, simply copy-paste the offered snippets into your `Snakemake <https://snakemake.github.io>`_ workflow.

The menu on the left (expand by clicking (+) if necessary), lists all available bioinformatics meta-wrappers.
